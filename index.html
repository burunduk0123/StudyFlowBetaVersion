Development Process

Documentation of product improvements from Alpha to Beta version.

1. Version Timeline

Alpha Version (March 2026)

Initial conceptual prototype of an investment tracker. Non-intuitive interface and lack of relevance to student needs.

Beta Version (April 2026)

Full transition to StudyFlow. Implementation of the calendar grid and automated progress calculation.

"Investment tracking is too complex for a school project. Focus on something students use daily, like homework."

User Testing:

"I want to see my workload distributed across the week, not just a simple list."
